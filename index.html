about me

My background as an artist brought me to website development. As a painter, photographer and potter I understand that good mastery of the tools and techniques of the craft are essential. To elevate a pottery piece to a piece of art, a snapshot to a memorable photograph worth of display, to render an oil painting with soul requires practice and knowledge. Art is not a gift we are given, it is the result of study, hard work and passion.


My scientific background led me to seek understanding of computer languages, software, and implementation of these tools correctly. With that in mind I embarked on learning the tools of the trade, becoming a full stack developer. I like to be able to work both with the front end and the back end of the process. I understand the technology to create a website, have the visual skills of an artist to make it beautiful and the ability to write content that expresses compelling messages.
